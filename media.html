Science Communication Philosophy

I believe that archaeological research has the power to connect people across cultures and time periods, fostering understanding and appreciation for our shared maritime heritage. Through various media platforms and public engagement initiatives, I work to make the fascinating world of underwater archaeology accessible to diverse audiences.

My approach to science communication emphasizes storytelling, visual engagement, and interactive learning experiences that bring the excitement of archaeological discovery to classrooms, communities, and online spaces worldwide.
